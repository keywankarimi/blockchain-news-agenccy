## 4. Main Risks and Regulatory Blockers

**CLARITY Act timing risk:** Senator Moreno warned explicitly that if it doesn't reach the Senate floor by May 2026, it may not move for years. A delay freezes institutional tokenization product development — companies cannot commit capital to token issuance structures without legal clarity on SEC vs. CFTC jurisdiction.

**GENIUS Act PPSI compliance complexity:** The 370-page OCC NPRM creates genuine operational burden — 100% liquid reserve backing, par redemption within 2 business days, $5M minimum capital, annual OCC exams, state-to-federal migration for issuers above $10B. Comments due May 1. Small stablecoin issuers may exit rather than comply.

**Bridge security:** Cumulative bridge hack losses exceed $2.8B. Seven blockchain security incidents in the Feb 23–Mar 1 window alone cost $13M, linked to oracle and cryptographic verification bugs. MiCA requires EU bridge operators to be licensed by July 2026 — compliance gap is significant.

**DeFi agent security:** No standard exists for how AI agents securely hold or use keys. $2B+ TVL is already being managed by agents. First major agent-wallet exploit will set back the DeFAI sector significantly.

**ZK centralization risk:** Core ZK proving infrastructure (Matter Labs, Succinct) is increasingly controlled by a small number of actors. If Prividium or similar platforms become critical banking infrastructure, concentration risk becomes a systemic concern.

## 5. New Startup Ideas and Product Wedges

**1. PPSI Compliance SaaS**
Build the compliance stack that stablecoin issuers must have by Jan 2027: reserve auditing automation, attestation generation, redemption workflow management, and OCC exam readiness dashboards. Hard deadline = non-discretionary spend. Target: Circle, Paxos, Ripple, bank subsidiary issuers. No dominant player yet.

**2. ZK-KYC Credential Layer for RWA Platforms**
ZK identity verification (prove accredited investor status, jurisdiction, AML clearance) without revealing personal data. Plug directly into tokenization platforms as a compliance module. Deutsche Bank + Privado ID proved the model. First company to get a second institutional deployment owns the standard.

**3. MiCA Bridge Compliance Wrapper**
EU bridge operators must be fully licensed as service providers by July 2026. Build a compliance middleware layer that wraps existing bridge infrastructure (LayerZero, Axelar, Stargate) with Travel Rule compliance, transaction monitoring, and MiCA reporting. B2B SaaS with a hard regulatory deadline and no dominant solution.

**4. Agent-Wallet Security Standard**
$2B+ TVL managed by AI agents, no security standard exists. Build the canonical framework: agent-specific key management, cosigned transaction approvals, on-chain permission scoping, and audit trails. Open-source the standard; monetize the managed service or enterprise audit tooling.

**5. RWA Secondary Market Liquidity Infrastructure**
Primary tokenization issuance is solved. Secondary trading is not. Build the ATS (Alternative Trading System) / dark pool infrastructure for tokenized private credit and institutional alternative funds — the layer between issuance and exit liquidity that institutions need before committing capital at scale.

**6. Stablecoin Treasury Ops Platform for SMBs**
Payoneer's OCC application confirms that SMB cross-border stablecoin banking is commercially viable. Build a treasury management and FX hedging layer for SMBs receiving stablecoin payments — automated conversion, multi-currency reserve management, compliance reporting. Target the $70M+ ARQ / KAST market segment from a software angle.
